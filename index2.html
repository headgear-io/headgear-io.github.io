About Virtuality

Virtual Reality is one of the single most important technological advances that has failed to deliver a viably consumer experience.
While blame can be cast in a multitude of directions (cumbersome hardware, unrealistic graphics, nonimmersion, unsuitable software, poor input controls, etc.), it is the human-computer interaction layer that best sums up all the hurdles facing head-mounted displays.
